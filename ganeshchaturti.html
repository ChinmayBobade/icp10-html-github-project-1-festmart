Ganesh Chaturti

 

Ganesh Chaturthi, also known as Vinayaka Chaturthi or Vinayaka Chavithi, is a 10-day Hindu festival that celebrates the birth of the elephant-headed god Ganesha.

 Here are some things that happen during Ganesh Chaturthi: Deities: Ganesha deities are set up in homes and public spaces. Prasad: Food blessed by the deity is distributed to the community. Meditation: People meditate on aspects of Ganesha's form. Fasts: Some people observe fasts. Modaks: Special "modaks" are made, which are said to be a favorite of Lord Ganesha. The festival begins on the fourth day of the Hindu month of Bhadrapada, which is usually in August or September. In 2024, Ganesh Chaturthi will be celebrated on Saturday, September 7.
